# S-043 Limited Beta Distribution and External Trial

## Status

Provisional post-S-042 scope. Not active or authorized.

## Objective

Prepare a deliberately limited beta or hosted demonstration for outside testers and collect bounded, privacy-conscious evidence.

## Dependency And Initiating Reason

Depends on successful S-037 through S-042 evidence and the S-036 readiness disposition.

## In Scope

- A constrained distribution or hosted demonstration for named trial goals and a limited audience.
- Clear prototype status, privacy, formats, limitations, setup/use, and feedback instructions.
- Reproducible release identity, rollback/withdrawal steps, and a bounded trial disposition.

## Codex Preparation

Prepare the smallest distribution checklist, verify release identity/documentation, and assemble a privacy-safe evidence packet without telemetry.

## Human Handoff

Approve the boundary, conduct the trial, review evidence, and choose a follow-up disposition.

## Permissible Narrow Work

Fix only reproduced blockers to the explicitly approved limited trial.

## Non-Goals

No production-readiness claim, accounts, analytics, scaling architecture, production-grade infrastructure, native packaging, public performance claim, or open public launch.

## Completion Boundary

The limited trial is reproducible, accurately documented, privacy-bounded, and human-dispositioned. No further phase activates automatically.
